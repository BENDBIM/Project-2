My Summer Project : KAMAT

Kamat is E-farming portal for the farmers to guide and provide necessary information about cultivation. This website is designed to provide knowledge to amateur farmers well as for the people who are earning their livelihood through agriculture.

This website provides various resources related to seeds, pesticide, fertilizers which are vital for farmers.

TThis project is visualized to make available relevant information and services to the farming community and private sector through the use of information technology.

Once you enter in Kamat, farmers can get access to relevant knowledge about the existing agricultural situation, seasonal farming, seeds, economic plant, important crops, and new plant.
